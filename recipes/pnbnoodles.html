Peanut butter noodles

Description

Delicious peanut butter noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodles of your choice
  • Peanut butter, crunchy
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Zucchini
  • Red pepper
  • Chili pepper
  • Sesame seeds
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Chop the zucchini, chili pepper and red pepper
  2. Mix the peanut butter, lime juice and soy sauce in a cup. Stir until smooth.
  3. Fry the vegetables in a pan
  4. Add the peanut butter sauce. Heat for a short while.
  5. Decorate with sesame seeds and cilantro
