Banana berry smoothie

Description

Refreshing banana berry smoothie to kickstart your day

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Quark
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Oat flakes
  • Oat drink

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a blender bowl
  2. Blend until smooth. Add oat drink and blend again in case the smoothie is too thick.
